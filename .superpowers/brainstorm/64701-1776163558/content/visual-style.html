What visual style for the floating objects?

These objects drift through space alongside your planets. How should they look?

A

Glowing Geometric Icons

Simple, abstract 3D shapes — a glowing shuttlecock silhouette, a neon drum outline, a wireframe dumbbell. Matches the space aesthetic with rim-glow like your planets. Low-poly, stylized, cohesive with the existing vibe. Easier to build.

B

Realistic 3D Models

Actual shuttlecocks, drum sticks, roller skates, footballs floating in space. More recognizable at a glance, but heavier to load and harder to make feel cohesive with the abstract planet aesthetic. Would need careful lighting to not clash.

C

Emoji-Style Sprites / 2D in 3D Space

Flat icons or stylized 2D sprites that always face the camera (billboards), floating and gently rotating. Think glowing emoji-like badges drifting through space. Lightweight, playful, easy to add new ones later.
