What happens when you discover an object?

A shuttlecock floats past... you hover or click it. What do you see?

A

Tooltip / Label Only

Hover reveals a small glass card with the interest name + a one-liner. E.g., "Badminton — District Level, Age 16." Quick, elegant, non-disruptive to the scroll flow. The object does a little spin or glow on hover.

B

Expand into a Mini Detail Card

Click opens a small floating glass card (like a mini version of your project cards) with the interest, your achievement, and maybe a fun line. E.g., "Tried to be a rapper at 16 — the bars were mid but the confidence was real." More personality, slightly more intrusive.

C

Collect Them All — Discovery Counter

Each object you find adds to a "discovered X/12" counter in the corner. Clicking reveals the detail card AND marks it as found. Gamifies the scroll — encourages visitors to explore the whole page to find all your interests. A subtle completionist hook.
