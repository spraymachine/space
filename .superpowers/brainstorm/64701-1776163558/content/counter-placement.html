Where should the discovery counter go?

The "Discovered 3/9" tracker that encourages visitors to find all your interests

A

Fixed Corner Badge

Small glass-morphism pill in the top-right or bottom-right corner. Always visible, updates with a subtle pulse animation when you find a new one. Minimal, stays out of the way.

B

Expanding Sidebar Tray

A small icon in the corner that expands on hover/click to show a grid of all 9 interests — found ones glow, undiscovered ones are silhouetted. Acts as both counter and checklist. More engaging but takes more screen space.

C

No Persistent Counter — Toast Notifications Only

No always-visible counter. Instead, a brief toast pops in when you discover one: "Found: Badminton 🏸 (3/9)". Disappears after 2 seconds. Cleaner UI, but visitors might not realize there are more to find.
